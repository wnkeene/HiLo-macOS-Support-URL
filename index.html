HiLo-Card for Mac and HiLoCard for iOS

Classic War Card Game for Mac and iOS

About the App

HiLo multiplatform app brings the classic War card game to your Mac, iPhone, and iPad with beautiful graphics, smooth gameplay, and elegant design. Perfect for a relaxing game session or a quick break from work.

Features

  • Single Player Mode - Challenge the house
  • Customizable Settings - Toggle jokers and sound effects
  • Native Mac Design - Built specifically for macOS
  • Beautiful Card Graphics - High-quality designs for Retina displays
  • Simple Gameplay - Easy-to-learn rules with intuitive controls
  • Score Tracking - Monitor your progress throughout each game

How to Play

You and the house are each dealt a card. The higher card wins the round and scores a point. Play continues until all cards are dealt. Simple, and satisfying!
